Welcome to Astra Vale - The Neon City

Astra Vale is a exansive neon megacity built on the ruins of an broken world powered by adavanced quantum technology that causes time itself to glitch. Towering chrome structures cut through glowing mist, while transit rails and drones weave throughout the skyline. The city never truly sleeps-its streets pulse with light,data, and underground networks of coders and fighters surviving in a place where seconds can stretch or snap without warning. Astra vale is very unique with the unstable flow of time giving people sharper reflexes and an edge in combat. Its a city with a balance bewteen technolgy and chaos.
